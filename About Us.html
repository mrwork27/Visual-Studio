About Us!

Catstone have been on a mission to find safe and loving homes for any and all abandoned animals- and we've been showcasing some of our furry friends through flyers and adverts for a long time now.



Rehoming cats has become the cornerstone of what we do, so why not try and bring the fluffy joy to more people? Welcome to our all new website, which will give even more people the opportunity to adopt their own bundle of love. Alongside this, we're reducing our impact on the environment by switching from our past use of paper flyers to giving all of our cats a space on the site to show off their personalities!


Why not check out our Find A Cat page to find out more about Luna, Cedric, Iris and the gang!
